The Lore of DARK CITY

DARK CITY is a completely different Roleplay experience from a normal basic roleplay Cities of now a day, a wide variety of activities and lots of ways to live as a character. Be the good guy or the bad guy, but make sure you leave your mark on the board.

DCPD

Dark City is proud of its Police department, they have hierarchy than citizens & gangs. Every citizen should respect and follow their lead, disrespecting or mocking PD may cause serious consequences. Powerful and responsible PD are the law enforcement team of the crime filled Dark City. Taking hostage of an on-duty officer is considered as FRP.

DCMD

The most important job in the city is of the Medical Department, let it be War or peace, Medics are always on the run, from accidents to intentional Knockouts, the DCMD has got your back. Taking hostage of an on-duty officer is considered as FRP.

DCPC

Not usual in most of the cities but the Dark City citizens and Government authorities run under the strict protocol mentioned in the DARK CITY PENAL CODE, the Penal code for every Citizen to be followed.

DCBC

With crime comes confusions, even if 100s of convicts are let free not a single innocent is to be punished, in there comes the Bar Council of Dark City. With proper enrolment and interviews, your visions to become a lawyer is not far away.

Families of Dark City

No city is complete without a family. Families have activities that they’re never even heard of or maybe imagined of, after the enrolment from the family protocol at the DCPD, every family has leader boards to put their names on, from business wars to arial warfare, we have it all in Dark City.

Syndicate

The days of Dark city are peaceful and enjoyable, jobs, hangouts, adventure, but behind what you see in the daylight, there is a hand of darkness controlling everything, the whole economy of the city are in their hands. They are known as the syndicate, they are the origin of illegals. Guns, Ammos, Drugs, and even war vehicles can be found in their inventory, but the Legal System are not capable of reaching them, only because the Chief is the one who runs the city. Citizens & Gangs should show respect & fear towards them. Their words are final.

Admins of Dark City

Let it be day or night, you are not alone in this city, need a help? Just call for the Admins, from anything to everything they could help you through, the ones with abilities that no citizen is capable of.

Vehicular Warfare

Dark city introduces a wide variety of vehicular warfare for the underworlds of the city, without a bit of fire arms and fun, what’s family for? conquer land, water and air to put your family name on the leader boards.

HUBs

If you thought that’s all, it’s not over yet, the inflow of illegals is through specific hubs in the city, and those hubs cannot be taken through agreement, you need a hub? You fight for it. You lose, they get the hub and the respect, every seller sells to the hub and every buyer buys from the hub, it’s that simple folk.

Outro

For the final touch, the City is a playground of endless possibilities, from night clubs, pink slip races and the PD, the Dark City is simply an entirely different experience.

Preamble

The Citizens of DARK CITY Having solemnly resolved to constitute Dark City into a Pure City of secularism, socialism, and democracy.

Every citizen of Dark City shall follow, obey, and respect the constitution and its penal code.

JUSTICE, social, economic, and political

LIBERTY of thought, expression, faith and worship

EQUALITY of status and opportunity

And to promote among them all

Fraternity assures an individual's dignity and unity and integrity of the city.

Any citizen acting against the preamble shall be treated and marked as a Traitor of DARK CITY and taken over by the Government of the City.

DCPC Section

Basic citizen rights

  • Right to food and water
  • Right to speak properly and respectfully (freedom of speech)
  • Right to look the way they like (freedom of appearance)
  • Right to privacy
  • Right to cluster (freedom of gathering)
  • Right to work
  • Right to Equality and Justice before Law
  • Right to purchase and sell, properly and rightfully (freedom to trade) not commercial
  • Right to security of self and owned properties in legal manner
  • Right to walk, ride, drive, swim or travel in any manner and everywhere except in areas restricted by the government (right to movement)

Rights regarding the gathering of citizens

  • Citizens shall gather at any legal parts of the city unless it is occupied by any other group or citizens in the proper manner
  • Citizens may gather themselves according to their likes and dislikes or their ideology
  • Citizens may gather according to their work
  • Citizens may gather according to their interest in properties, games, vehicles, entertainment, and places
  • Citizens are allowed to join or leave the so-called gatherings and may follow the rules of those gatherings if any (applicable only for DARK CITY recognized gatherings / Clubs)

Infractions regarding traffic rules

  • Absence of valid Registration / Number Plate

    • If a citizen is found driving a vehicle without a valid registration displayed outside the car at a designated spot to be noticed by everyone, the citizen shall be pressed charges of traffic infraction under DCPC section 101 and charged a sum of 10,000 as a penalty alongside impounding the vehicle on failing to pay the sum, the vehicle shall be released after the sum is paid to the government.

  • Speed violation

    • If a citizen is found driving a vehicle above the designated speed limit of the respective road he is traveling and is traveling at a speed of over 200 kph, the citizen shall be charged with a traffic violation under DCPC section 102 and subjected to a penalty of 35,000

  • Speed violation over 200 kph

    • If a citizen is found to be driving a vehicle above 200 kph at any road in the city, the citizen shall be pressed charges under DCPC section 103 and subjected to a penalty of 40,000

  • Vehicle ramming

    • If a citizen is found ramming a vehicle onto other vehicles, properties, or persons, the citizen shall be pressed charges under DCPC section 104 and subjected to a penalty of 50,000, alongside impounding the vehicle for one hour.

  • Public nuisance

    • If a citizen is found Performing any activity using a vehicle which is a threat to others or a public nuisance such as Wheelie, Burnout, Stoppie, Drift, Anti lag, Revving, Sound Pollution, Donuts, double wheel balancing in public roads, the citizen in action shall be pressed charges under DCPC section 105 and subjected to a penalty of 45,000 alongside Impounding the Vehicle in action for 1 hour. If the citizen is doing so in his/her personal private property or if not a threat to the public, the charges may not be pressed against

  • Misusing of Horn

    • If a citizen is using a horn in an unwanted manner, causing public nuisance or difficulty for other citizens of the city, the government officials, or at government properties, the citizen in action shall be charged under DCPC section 106 and subjected to a 20,000 penalty and 1 hour's Impound for the vehicle used on the spot

  • Illegal Parking

    • If a Vehicle is found parked at a place where parking is not allowed or exceeds the maximum time allowed to park the vehicle at the particular spot, the vehicle shall be sent into civilian impound, pressing charges under DCPC section 107 and subjected to a penalty of 20,000

  • Driving without valid License

    • If a citizen is found driving or riding a vehicle without a valid driving license or an expired driver’s license, the citizen in action shall be pressed charges under DCPC section 108, subjecting the citizen to a penalty of 40,000 and impounding the vehicle used. The impounded vehicle should be returned only if the citizen in action provides a valid driver’s license.

  • Drunk and Drive

    • If a citizen is found driving under the influence of alcohol, the citizen found in action should be subjected to a breath analyser test, and if found guilty, the citizen shall be pressed charges under DCPC section 109 and subjected to a penalty of 55,000 and the citizen shall be kept under police custody until sober

  • Evading arrest in Vehicle

    • If a citizen evades arrest for a proper reason or during a situation or from a crime scene, the citizen shall be caught and pressed charges under DCPC section 110 subjecting the citizen in action to a penalty of 99,000 minimum up to 200,000 according to the severity of infractions during the evading process which includes damages occurred to government properties without the intervention of the police

Roleplay Rules

IMPORTANT RULES

If you think the opposing player is intentionally trying to provoke you. You must record your POV and do not provoke them back to create a situation. In extreme case situations and with a solid RP reason, if you have to take an officer as a hostage, make sure there are at least 8 other officers on duty. During any situation, if you aim at a police officer or give a warning fire to the police officer, they can shoot you down directly without any prior warning. If you want to start a shooting scenario but the other party isn't aware, you'll have to give him a chance by suppressing fire and giving them time to react to the shootout. Suppressing fire does not mean firing one bullet into the sky and the other into the head. You must give time to clear the situation or count till 30 before killing them. NOTE : If a family/gang declares open war on another team/gang, you do not need to give warning fire.

You should not use the vale service to retrieve your vehicle during an active situation. You cannot kidnap or take a fight with a person for silly reasons like unknowingly pitting your vehicle, etc.

No one is allowed to use voice chat or /me,/say,etc while dead; if this is reported, it will be considered a fail RP. You can use ooc chat only for informing ems services or any moderators if you are lacking ems services. PD vs Citizen Rules

Firing Towards PD (Safe Zone)
Firing towards the PD or Safe Zone is considered Fail Roleplay (FRP).

Search and Suspicion
Police have the right to search both you and your vehicle if they suspect you or your vehicle is involved in criminal activity. It is your responsibility to prove your innocence.

Arrest with Arrest Warrant
PD has the right to arrest a player once a government-issued arrest warrant is in place, regardless of location (Safe Zone, Gang House, City Limits).

Involvement in Robbery or Shooting
If a player is not part of an ongoing robbery or shooting situation, they should not intervene. Interfering in these situations will be considered FRP.

Illegal Weapon Possession
If you are caught with an illegal weapon in public, the PD has the authority to handcuff you and confiscate the weapon.
